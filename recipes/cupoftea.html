A Proper Cup of Tea

This is dedicated to Brioni-Leigh Mackinnon. A woman that, while I love, does not know how to make a cup of tea. This project then is dedicated to helping her achieve her greatness in tea-making. The art of tea blending is not all that complex, but I imagine her huge brain has been used primarily for the intake of tooth nonce subjects, and has not been utilized solely to make best cup of tea possible; and so for this reason, I will let her off.

However, now she must learn how to properly make a cup of tea and it does not, in any way include dipping toast into it. If I even suspect you are using this recipe to dip toast into, I will take it down with good conscience.
